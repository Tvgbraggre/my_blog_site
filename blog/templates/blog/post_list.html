    	it is a real possession in the changing fortunes
    	of time.
    	Exercise caution in your business affairs;
    	for the world is full of trickery.

    	is; many persons strive for high ideals; and everywhere
    	life is full of heroism. Be yourself. Especially,
    	do not feign affection. Neither be cynical about love;
    	fo in the face of all aridity and disenchantment it is
    	perennial as grass. Take kindly the counsel of the years
    	gracefully surrendering the things of youth.
    	Nurture strength of spirit to shield you in sudden misfortune.
    	But do not distress yourself with imaginings. many fears are born
    	of fatigue and loneliness. Beyond a wholesome discipline,

    	And wether or not it is clear to you, no doubt the universe
    	is unfolding as it should. Therefore be at peace with God,
    	whatever you conceive Him to be, and whatever you
    	labors and aspirations, in the noisy
    	confusion of life keep peace with
    	your soul.
    	With all its sham, drudgery
    	and broken dreams, it

    	Happy!
